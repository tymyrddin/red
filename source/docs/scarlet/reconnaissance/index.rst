Casing the clacks
=========================================================

.. image:: /_static/images/tree-recon.png
   :alt: Underground recon

Before a message is nudged, the towers are watched. Reconnaissance is the patient part: the quiet weeks of
reading which Grand Trunk a city leans on, learning each relay's normal hum, and finding the one whose
misbehaviour would read as weather rather than sabotage.
