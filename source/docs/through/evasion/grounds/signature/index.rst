Breaking the mould: Evading digital fingerprints
==========================================================

Security systems often rely on known signatures to detect threats. This section shows how to alter the
code's characteristics, avoiding detection by static and behavioural signatures, much like a raccoon altering
its path to avoid leaving a trail.
