The Primordial Grimoire
=========================================

A grimoire typically contains spells, rituals, incantations, magical recipes, and sometimes instructions for
summoning or communicating with spirits. It can also include astrological charts, alchemical formulas, and
protective charms. A manual of magical knowledge.

This one covers offensive doctrine: the categories of move that constitute network attack practice. Recon,
manipulation, intrusions and the rest. Each entry describes a technique type rather than a step-by-step procedure;
those live in Tradecraft. The moves get applied, in combination, in :doc:`Earthworks <../../../earthworks/index>`.
