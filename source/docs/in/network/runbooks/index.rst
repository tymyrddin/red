Tradecraft
======================================================

Deterministic, task-oriented guides. Each runbook covers a specific operation: reconnaissance, wireless, layer 2,
name resolution, C2 tunnelling, lateral movement, BGP routing. These are the constituent moves. The attack surfaces
they operate against are modelled in Rootways and Patches. Playbooks compose them into chains. :doc:`Earthworks <../../../earthworks/index>` applies them in scenarios.
