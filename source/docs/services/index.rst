Myrddin's menagerie
======================================================

.. image:: /_static/images/myrddins-menagerie.png
  :alt: A wild forest with foxes, raccoons, squirrels, and falcons, interacting with glowing digital artefacts, signal flares, hidden traps, and networked trees.

The workshops here are built around a consistent premise: the gap between how a system is believed
to work and how it actually works is where the interesting problems live. Each one is designed to
make that gap concrete, something participants can encounter directly rather than read about.
