UU Power & Light masterclass
======================================================

.. image:: /_static/images/ot-before.png

Finding critical vulnerabilities in industrial systems is easy. Convincing leadership to fix them is not.

In this masterclass, you either bring an existing PoC or make a convincing PoC using the simulator. Then you have sixty
minutes to brief the people who matter.

Your audience includes UU Power & Light’s leadership and, ultimately, Lord Vetinari himself. They will ask about cost,
operational impact, regulatory exposure, and whether you are quite certain you understand how their city actually
functions.

*“Just unplug it” will not be accepted. Neither will “best practice says”.*

This roleplay exercise teaches the skill most security professionals never get trained in: translating technical risk
into operationally grounded decisions that survive scrutiny and result in funding, not polite nods.

Run standalone as a reporting and decision-making exercise, or as the final act following discovery -> defend -> report.
