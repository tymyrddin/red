Unseen University Power & Light Co.
======================================================

.. image:: /_static/images/power-light.png
   :alt: Steampunk industrial city scene, showing the Unseen University Power & Light Co. (UU P&L) facilities in Ankh-Morpork. Intricate brass machinery, steam pipes, glowing arcane energy conduits.

Follow the adventures of testing the industrial control systems at the Unseen University Power & Light Co. (UU P&L),
Ankh-Morpork's main supplier of electricity, hot water, and occasional mysterious glows. Their facilities include:

- The Hex Steam Turbine Control System (legacy 1970s PLCs)
- The Bursar's Automated Alchemical Reactor Controls
- The Library Environmental Management System (temperature-critical for L-space stability)
- The City-Wide Distribution SCADA (manages the entire Ankh-Morpork grid)
