Unseen University Power & Light Co.
======================================================

.. image:: /_static/images/ot-company-location.png
   :alt: Steampunk industrial city scene, showing the Unseen University Power & Light Co. (UU P&L) facilities in Ankh-Morpork. Intricate brass machinery, steam pipes, glowing arcane energy conduits.

Follow the adventures of testing the industrial control systems at the Unseen University Power & Light Co. (UU P&L),
Ankh-Morpork's main supplier of electricity, hot water, and occasional mysterious glows. This story
served as a user story for developing workshops and analytical processes, and for building a simulator to practise
against. The first simulator proved not dynamic enough for the scenarios we had in mind, so work is
now under way on a more capable ICS SimLab.
